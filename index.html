My PPL Routine

5-day Push / Pull / Legs · 80 kg lifter · no barbell/dumbbell rows

The things that make it work

  • Warm up before the first heavy lift: 2–3 ramp-up sets (e.g. empty bar → 40 → 55 → working weight).
  • Progressive overload is the whole game. Each week add a little weight or 1 more rep. Hit the top of the rep range on all sets → bump the weight next session.
  • Rest: 2–3 min on heavy compounds (bench, squat, OHP, pulldown), 60–90 sec on the smaller stuff.
  • Recovery: ~130–160 g protein/day (1.6–2 g/kg) and 7+ hours sleep.
  • Weights are starting estimates — if the last 1–2 reps aren't genuinely hard, go up; if you can't hit the low rep with clean form, go down.
