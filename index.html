Make it work

  • Warm up before the first heavy lift: 2–3 ramp-up sets.
  • Progressive overload — each week add a little weight or 1 more rep. Top of the rep range on all sets → bump the weight.
  • Rest: 2–3 min on heavy compounds, 60–90 sec on the small stuff.
  • Recovery: ~130–160 g protein/day and 7+ hours sleep.
